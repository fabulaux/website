What We Do

Fabula means "story" in Latin. We are storytellers who innovate through food, technology, and design to create multisensory experiences and give back to humanity through sponsored events. Our partners are those who want to be heard through the collaboration of form factors representing sight, taste, touch, smell and hearing.

Design Process

Previous Next

Our latest case study was on graffiti art. After visiting the most renown graffiti spots in downtown Los Angeles and observing artists in action, we wanted to capture the essence of graffiti through the medium of food. To us, food is a vehicle for triggering memories through flavors and textures, so we designed and prototyped this installation: a three-course meal that depicts a story celebrating getting back to our childhood roots.
